About Us

A solution-oriented, can-do commercial roofing company with decades of experience in Central Texas.

Dean Contracting Company, Inc. started as a sole proprietorship in 1987 by founder Tracey Dean. The company was incorporated on March 28, 1990 for the purpose of doing business as a commercial roofing and sheet metal contractor.

The decades of experience reflected by the experienced individuals in our management team has given us a reputation in our area as a "solution oriented — can do" company. We excel at both new and re-roofing projects requiring commercial metal, built up, single ply, modified bitumen, tile, shingle, green roof, and plaza deck systems.

In addition to doing all types of roofing, siding, soffit, and waterproofing, we are also certified installers of LiveRoof hybrid plant and paver systems.

We set ourselves apart from other roofing contractors by having an impeccable safety record; meeting project schedules and budgets; and a verifiable reputation in the industry for consistent quality work and dedication to meeting the needs of our clients.

Please browse our website to see examples of the work we have completed and other detailed information that outlines the capabilities and experience we have. Let us know how we can help you!
